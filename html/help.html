What is Join?

Join is a Kanban project management tool that allows all team members to easily create, share, edit and finish tasks.
In a very clear way it is shown which tasks have to be done with which priority.

How to use it?

1.

Start by creating a task.
You can do this in the Add Task section.

2.

Now take a look at the board. Here you can find the task you just created.
You can drag and drop the task into different categories.

3.

You want to see all contact details of your team members at a glance? No problem!
Switch to the Contacts section and create a new contact with all relevant contact information.

4.

If you want to quickly see which task it is better to do first, take a look at the Summary section.
Here you will find a small view of how many tasks are on the board and with which priority.
